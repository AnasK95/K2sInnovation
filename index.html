Why Solar

What we have created

Solar Rooftop

Solar rooftop can be installed in a commercial space for generation of electricity. It has helped many offices across the globe to save expenses on the energy bill drastically. The system sizes varies anywhere between 10kW to 100kW depending upon the requirements. In a long run, these system are sustainable, affordable and profitable. Installers have the right to feed solar electricity into the public grid and hence receive a reasonable premium tariff per generated kWh reflecting the benefits of solar electricity to compensate for the current extra costs of PV electricity.

Solar Power Plant

Solar power plant refers to installation of PV module on a very large scale for solar energy production. Areas such as barren and deserted land can be well utilized for setting up of power plant. It creates electric energy by transforming vacant or even underused land into farms that produce energy. India which receives sunlight almost throughout the year is suitable for large scale power production. On a long run, solar energy production is the future. Government of India is supporting financial resources to fund projects to put up community solar farms as part of their energy development programs. Reduction in cost of PV technology further gives a boost in making solar plant a reality.

Solar Water Heater

Solar thermal collectors capture and retain heat from the sun and use it to heat a liquid. It is a cost effective way to generate hot water for your home as the fuel used is free of cost. They are relatively easy to build and install making them quite popular. We deal in Evacuated Tube Collectors (ETC) based Solar Water Heaters. All products are government approved and equipped with latest technology. There are wide range of solar water heater based on tube dimensions and System Volume with life of around 15-20 years

Solar Steet Light

Solar Street Lighting System is a lighting system for the illumination of streets and cross roads located in areas that are not connected to the power grid. The system is controlled by a controller with automatic ON/OFF time switch, which control the light from dusk till morning or other setting time. This system is provided with battery backup for 12-15 hours daily. Every solar street light unit comes as an independent kit. Application Outdoor lightning , pedestrian streets, Residential colonies , parking lots , remote areas, gardens.

Solar Power Pump

A solar-powered pump is a pump running on electricity generated by photovoltaic panels to lift water for irrigation, horticulture farms, gardens, drinking and other applications. The operation of solar powered pumps is more economical mainly due to the lower operation and maintenance costs. Solar pumps are useful where grid electricity is unavailable or there is scarcity of power supply. A solar powered pump system has solar panels, the controller and the pump. The size of the PV- system is directly dependent on the size of the pump, the amount of water that is required (m3/d) and the solar irradiance available.

Home Light System

Solar home systems (SHS) are stand-alone photovoltaic systems that offer a cost-effective mode of supplying amenity power for lighting and appliances to remote off-grid households. A SHS typically includes one or more PV modules consisting of solar cells, a charge controller which distributes power and protects the batteries and appliances from damage and at least one battery to store energy for use when the sun is not shining..
